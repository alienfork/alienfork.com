Refund Policy

Last updated: August 15, 2025

For digital goods purchased directly from us, refund requests are considered within 14 days of purchase if the game has not been substantially played. A game is considered substantially played if more than 2 hours of gameplay have been logged or if it has been launched more than once. For subscriptions, you can cancel anytime; cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.

If you purchased our games through a third-party platform (such as Steam, itch.io, or any console marketplace), refund requests must follow that platform’s refund process and policies, including their timelines. We cannot process those refunds directly.

To request a refund for a direct purchase, email support@alienfork.com with your order email, receipt ID, and reason. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method. We reserve the right to refuse a refund if we believe the request is fraudulent or abusive.
